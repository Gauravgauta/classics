History

Foundation Years

Classic Residency, Haridwar

Classic Hotels began its journey with the launch of Classic Residency in Haridwar, marking its entry into the hospitality industry. Positioned as a mid-scale leisure hotel, it catered to pilgrims and tourists visiting the holy city. The property became known for its expansive lawns, warm hospitality, and convenient proximity to Har Ki Pauri, making it a preferred choice for spiritual travelers.

Strengthening Hill Presence

Classic The Mall, Nainital

Continuing its growth, Classic Hotels acquired and rebranded a heritage property on Nainital’s iconic Mall Road. Classic The Mall was crafted to appeal to leisure tourists, offering picturesque lake-view stays while retaining its old-world charm.

Expansion to Hills

Classic Hill Top Resort, Chamba

Venturing deeper into Uttarakhand’s serene landscapes, Classic Hotels launched Classic Hill Top Resort in Chamba. Designed for both leisure and corporate guests, the resort boasts modern amenities, a swimming pool, multi-cuisine dining, and versatile event spaces ideal for conferences, retreats, and destination weddings. Its colonial-style charm and serene surroundings make it a perfect hill getaway.

Entry into Delhi NCR

Hotel Diplomat, Delhi

Marking its foray into the capital, Classic Hotels took over and rebranded Hotel Diplomat in New Delhi’s prestigious diplomatic enclave. Tailored for business travelers and foreign dignitaries, the hotel offered an upscale boutique experience with personalized services. Its strategic location and curated luxury made it a preferred address for discerning guests visiting Delhi.
